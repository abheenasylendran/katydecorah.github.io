Hello! I’m Katy DeCorah. I’m a front-end web developer and designer from Albany, New York. Currently, I'm a UI designer at Fire Engine RED.

I love treats, being outdoors, making seasonal playlists, and tinkering on the web.

I built, and work to improve, Static Map Maker to help people navigate the Google and Mapbox static map API. It’s my tinker-with-AngularJS project.

This site is built with Jekyll, Sass, Grunt, and hosted on Github. I also use CodePen embeds, Mapbox maps, FontAwesome icons, Typekit fonts, and Flat UI Colors. And my images are hosted Flickr.
